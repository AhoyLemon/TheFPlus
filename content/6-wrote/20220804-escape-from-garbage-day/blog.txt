On **September 17th 2022**, The Ridiculists will escape From Garbage Day.

### [And here is the schedule](https://ahoylemon.notion.site/Garbage-Day-2022-Schedule-Public-1b366e6d4fac419b840080c60dbb1157)

We've done this multiple times now, and The F Plus still haven't learned this is a bad idea: We will be broadcasting live for **24 Terrible Hours**, reading a new document with a new theme every hour, all the while raising money for [The National Network of Abortion Funds](https://abortionfunds.org). We've scheduled dozens of readers, a multitude of artists, prizes and other nonsense that only a sleep-deprived mind can think of. It will be exhausting. It will be fun. And hopefully we'll generate a lot of money for a cause that needs it more than ever.

[Join us](https://www.addevent.com/event/Iq14591473), starting at Noon EDT, as we Escape From Garbage Day, and make the future look a little less shit.
