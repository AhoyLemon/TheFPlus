Title: Submission Hopper

----

Text: 

Below is a complete list of of all the [submitted](/submit) content we've currently received, listed from most recent to least recent. Everything in this list is directly pulled from the master spreadsheet of content we look at when we're getting ready to record a new episode. Ridiculists tend to score different pieces depending on what they personally think of the document itself, and each piece gets an internal score. Submitted content will stick around in this list until...
1. It's recorded and thus moved to a separate spreadsheet of episodes that need to be edited and released. Or...
2. It's decided that this particular episode probably won't get recorded and so is brought to [the dump](/submit/dump) where people can check out the documents that didn't quite make it.

Please try to not have hurt feelings if #2 happens to you. We get a lot of terrific submissions, and it seems better to distribute the content to anyone who may be interested than to let things languish unread.
