Text: 

As donation incentives for the last three [Garbage Days](https://thefpl.us/find/tag:garbage%20day), our own [Jimmyfranks](https://thefpl.us/meet/jimmyfranks) took it upon himself to create *extremely* limited edition toys as donation incentives, and they've been as lovely as they have been unexpected.

* For 2018, it was announced that the top donator would receive an "action fetish", with no more information. We went through the donation roster later, and Jimmyfranks sent out 12 [feet](#1) and 12 [cars](#2).
* In 2019, nearing the end of the marathon, Jimmyfranks announced that he would be giving something special to anyone who donated $150 or more. Some time later, they received their very own [Roy Orbison wrapped in Clingfilm](#3). Jimmyfranks also had some [extras](#4) that he gave to Lemon, who gave them out willy nilly.
* And then in 2020, we were *very* clear: In the last hour, we told people "If you donate $100 or more, Jimmyfranks will send you a rock." And 28 people did. So they got [Pat-Rock 82](#5)

Costs for materials and shipping all of this were paid for by The F Plus' own funds, and the artists involved provided their own work gratis, simply because it's fun to do something so absurd.

Will more F Plus toys be made in the future? Maybe!
