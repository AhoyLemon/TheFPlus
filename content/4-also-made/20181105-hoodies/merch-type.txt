Text: 

Rep The Internet in style and comfort with these French Terry Hoodies!

We had the idea of doing hoodies for a couple years, but it didn't seem feasible until we met Jessi at Afternoon Printing who was as excited as we were to carry the idea through. These middleweight hoodies are incredibly comfortable, well suited to layering, and because of the French Terry (55% cotton / 45% poly) won't pill up like a fleecier hoodie would. Please believe me, if there's one piece of clothing I have opinions on, it's hoodies.

The F+ logo was printed in soft ink using a multi-stage process over the side, with I'M FROM THE INTERNET offseting it from behind. The right side pocket contains a hidden third pocket inside, as well as a headphone pass-thru. The drawcord tips are made of nickel. Also, these things have **reinforced thumb holes**!
