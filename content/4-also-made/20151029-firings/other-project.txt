After a recent round of layoffs at Twitter, we had a conversation about what kinds of jobs would be expendable in the new economy. Then Lemon bought a domain name and banged this thing out in about a day.

It's powered with javascript, and the full source is [available on GitHub](https://github.com/AhoyLemon/sf-firings).  If you want to dig around in the code and add your own job titles and messages, we are happy to accept pull requests.
